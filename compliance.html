Legal & Regulatory Compliance Statement

Effective Date: 8 December 2025

Molexi Fortuna Private Limited is committed to operating in an ethical, transparent and lawful manner. This Compliance Statement outlines our alignment with applicable laws and regulatory principles.

1. Indian Legal Framework

We operate in accordance with the laws of India, including but not limited to:

  • Information Technology Act, 2000 and applicable Rules
  • Indian Contract Act, 1872
  • Companies Act, 2013
  • Consumer Protection Act, 2019
  • Relevant cyber and data protection provisions

2. Data Protection & Privacy

We follow privacy and data protection principles inspired by:

  • Indian IT (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011
  • Global best practices similar to GDPR principles (lawful purpose, consent, limited retention, user rights)

Details about how we collect, use, store and protect personal data are provided in our Privacy Policy.

3. Financial Market and SEBI-Related Compliance

Molexi Fortuna Private Limited is a financial education and research-oriented company. We:

  • Do not provide SEBI-registered investment advisory services
  • Do not provide stock tips, trading recommendations or portfolio management
  • Do not solicit funds for investment or trading purposes
  • Clearly declare that all content is for educational purposes only

Users are strongly encouraged to consult SEBI-registered intermediaries for any financial, trading or investment decisions.

4. Advertising and Communication Compliance

In our marketing, advertising and public communication, we endeavor to:

  • Avoid misleading, exaggerated or false claims
  • Avoid any guarantee of returns or profits
  • Provide clear disclaimers about the nature of our services
  • Maintain transparency in pricing (where applicable) and offerings

Our communication is intended to be consistent with the policies of major platforms such as Google Ads, Meta (Facebook/Instagram), and other digital channels, where used.

5. Content Standards and Ethics

We aim to ensure that all educational content:

  • Is research-oriented and not speculative advice
  • Does not promote irresponsible trading behavior
  • Emphasizes risk awareness and financial responsibility

6. Grievance Redressal
